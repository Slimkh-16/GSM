A word from the Managing Director

“GMS was founded in 2006 in Switzerland and this year we are celebrating our 10th anniversary as a global company. Our core expertise is messaging, irrespectively of the device, place and messenger in use. Our main focus is the future, a connected world where everyone can reach and be reached with important and sensitive information through secured channels. We believe that a Connected world is of immense value and work passionately to make this dream come true.”

Iurii Makarenko Managing Director
GMS

Today GMS is a leading international telecommunications company, specializing in mobile messaging business, mobile advertising and complex IT solutions for Telecommunications Operators and Business Enterprises across the world. GMS offers its products and value-added telecommunication services in more than 200 countries, supplying our clients with innovative services and solutions. We are very proud to count more than 400 companies as our partners and customers worldwide.

Focusing on continuous development, GMS has expanded its services to the international market and has taken key steps towards a unified offering of messaging solutions for both Telecom Operators and Business Enterprises alike. The current GMS’ profile includes long-term and successful partnerships with the foremost network operators and SMS-hubs worldwide. As an associate member, the Company has entered one of the principle network associations GSMA, which unites nearly 800 operators with more than 400 companies in the broader mobile ecosystem.

The key aspects for clients and partners in cooperation with GMS are
  • 1 Maximized Opportunities Providing a complete Mobile Messaging solution for any customer and telecommunications operator, we incorporate the full spectrum of services, from extended messaging via SMS, MMS, OTT, PUSH, Email, Encrypted messages with our hybrid messaging platform to mobile advertising expertise and complex IT solutions: web-based and mobile applications development
  • 2 Maximized Reach Wide service offerings are available for any customer and telecommunications operator around the globe
  • 3 Maximized Reliability High performance, redundant and flexible messaging platform ensures availability and service level commitment of 99.5%
  • 4 Maximized Security Encrypted channels and state-of-the-art network anti-spam protection qualified by GMS ISO 27001 Certificate
  • 5 Maximized Availability Continuous monitoring of online statistics and 24/7 technical support. Our dedicated Account Management provides an individual approach to our customers’ needs.
